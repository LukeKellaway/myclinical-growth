Why MyClinical Growth exists

NHS procurement opportunities and UK digital health funding are scattered across dozens of portals, frameworks and funders. The information is public and free — it just isn't surfaced anywhere a founder would reliably see it. A £750m framework can land with near-invisible communications, and the companies that should be bidding find out too late.

MyClinical Growth is the surfacing layer. It aggregates every source, adds a plain-English read on what each opportunity actually means, and delivers it as a daily brief. It is free to browse, open by design, and built so UK healthtech gets a fair shot at the table. It is part of the MyClinical line.

How the data works

  • Find a Tender and Contracts Finder are pulled automatically via their open data APIs, filtered to NHS and health-sector buyers with a digital-health signal.
  • Frameworks, grants and the wider directory are tracked and maintained editorially — many of these sources have no machine-readable feed.
  • The "what it means" notes are editorial judgement, not automated text. That is the part that turns a notice into a decision.
  • Everything links back to the original source. We aggregate and signal; we don't replace the official portals.

Privacy policy

This is a starting draft. Have it reviewed before launch.

Browsing MyClinical Growth requires no account and collects no personal data. If you subscribe to the daily brief, we collect your email address for the sole purpose of sending you that brief. The lawful basis is your consent, given when you subscribe.

Your email is stored in Mailchimp, our email provider. We never share, sell or rent the list. Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe, and you can ask us to delete your details at any time. We don't use tracking cookies or third-party advertising.

Questions, corrections, or a source we've missed — get in touch and we'll sort it.
