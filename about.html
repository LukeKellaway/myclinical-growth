About

A fair shot at the table

Why this exists, how the data works, and how we handle your information.

Why MyClinical Growth exists

NHS procurement opportunities and UK digital health funding are scattered across dozens of portals, frameworks and funders. The information is public and free. It just isn't surfaced anywhere a founder would reliably see it. A £900m framework can land with near-invisible communications, and the companies that should be bidding find out too late.

MyClinical Growth is the surfacing layer. It aggregates every source, adds a plain-English read on what each opportunity actually means, and delivers it as a daily brief and a weekly roundup. It is free to browse, open by design, and built so UK healthtech gets a fair shot at the table. It is part of the MyClinical line.

How the data works

  • Find a Tender and Contracts Finder are pulled automatically via their open data APIs, filtered to NHS and health-sector buyers with a digital-health signal.
  • Frameworks, grants and the wider directory are tracked and maintained editorially. Many of these sources have no machine-readable feed.
  • The "what it means" notes are editorial judgement, not automated text. That is the part that turns a notice into a decision.
  • Everything links back to the original source. We aggregate and signal; we don't replace the official portals.

Privacy policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

The short version. We collect your email if you subscribe. We do not track you, sell your data, or share the list. You can unsubscribe in one click from any email. Full details below.

Who we are

MyClinical Growth is a free aggregation resource for UK NHS procurement and digital health funding. It is part of the MyClinical line, operated from the United Kingdom. The data controller is Luke Kellaway, contactable at info@myclinical.co.uk.

What we collect, and when

Browsing the site collects no personal data. We do not run analytics, we do not use third-party advertising, and we do not set tracking cookies (see Cookies below).

If you subscribe to the brief (daily or weekly), we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your daily / weekly preferences
  • The timestamp of your subscription and any subsequent open or click events recorded by our email provider

If you ask for bid-writing help on the Bid Help page, or submit the interest form on the Capital page, we collect:

  • Your name, email address, company or firm name, and contact details
  • Details about the opportunity or tender you share, and anything you add in the message field

Where you ask for bid-writing help, we pass these details to the bid writer we connect you with, so they can contact you. We do not share them more widely.

If you send a tip via the Submit page, we collect any details you choose to enter, including an optional name and email if you want a reply.

Why we collect it (lawful basis)

For the brief (daily and/or weekly): your consent, given when you tick the subscribe form. You can withdraw it at any time using the one-click unsubscribe in every email.

For the interest and tip forms: legitimate interest in evaluating and contacting prospective directory partners, and in maintaining the accuracy of the resource. You can ask us to delete your submission at any point.

Cookies

We do not set advertising or analytics cookies. The only cookies set on this site come from the technical infrastructure that serves the pages (Netlify) and the Mailchimp forms once you submit one, and are limited to what is strictly necessary to deliver the service you asked for. Under UK PECR, strictly-necessary cookies do not require a consent banner. If we ever add analytics or tracking, we will add a proper consent banner first.

Where your data lives

Email subscribers are stored in Mailchimp (Intuit Inc., US-based). Mailchimp's privacy policy: intuit.com/privacy/statement.

Interest and tip form submissions are stored in Netlify Forms (Netlify Inc., US-based) and forwarded to our MyClinical inbox. Netlify's privacy policy: netlify.com/privacy.

For any personal data leaving the UK or EEA, we rely on the standard contractual clauses provided by these vendors as the transfer mechanism, plus the UK adequacy framework where it applies.

How long we keep it

Email subscriptions are kept for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, we remove your address from active sending lists and retain only the minimum suppression record required to honour your unsubscribe.

Interest and tip form submissions are kept for up to 24 months from the date of submission, then deleted, unless we are in active conversation with you.

What we never do

  • We never sell, rent or share your email address with third parties.
  • We never use your email for retargeting, advertising profiles, or any non-MyClinical-Growth purpose.
  • We never enrich your data with third-party sources (no LinkedIn lookup, no firmographic enrichment).
  • We never use AI to process your personal data in a way that makes decisions about you.

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, port or object to the processing of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email info@myclinical.co.uk and we will respond within one month.

If you believe we have mishandled your data, you can complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint. We would rather you came to us first so we can fix it.

Changes to this policy

We will update this page when our practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. If a change materially affects what we do with subscriber data, we will email subscribers before it takes effect.

Get in touch

Questions, corrections, a source we have missed, or a feature request: email info@myclinical.co.uk and we will sort it.
