Referrals & Appointments national funding
NHS England · £15m to £20m
Not a tender. A trust-facing pot from NHS England to fund adoption of existing national platforms (BaRS, e-RS, NHS App). Deadline is 8 days away. The trusts that win these are the ones who already have a supplier lined up and a delivery plan signed by a clinical SRO. If you supply into any of these stacks, your trust customers should already know whether they're bidding. Call them this week. If you don't have those relationships, the window is too short for new ones.

Clinical systems & EPR
Digital Medicines Management national funding
NHS England · Not disclosed
Follow-on to the digital medicines first-of-type schemes. Trust-facing on paper, supplier-dependent in practice. Applications expect evidence of supplier commitment, the commercial approach, and partnership with other trusts on the same stack. That last point is the tell: NHS England wants supplier-anchored consortia, not lone trusts. EPMA, GP Connect specialists, and dispensing-automation vendors should be the ones starting these conversations, not the trusts. Applications open early June and close 17 July at 5pm. If you supply EPMA or closed-loop stack, get three or more trust customers aligned in the next two weeks.

Healthcare AI Solutions Framework
NHS Shared Business Services · £900,000,000
Updated 23 May: value lifted to £900m incl VAT, up from the original £150m estimate and the £750m figure carried earlier. A six-fold jump after supplier engagement that signals NHS SBS is expecting heavy lot-level competition. The procurement route for UK healthcare AI for the next four years. Lots 6 and 7 (operational efficiency, advisory) remain the quieter opportunity. Enquiry deadline 29 May, tender deadline 23 June. Tight: clinical evidence packs and DCB0129 documentation take weeks.

Condition-specific
Wellcome Prize for Mental Health Science (with Nature)
Wellcome Trust · $1m winner, three finalists at $250k each
Prize, not a grant. Recognition plus cash for work that's already advanced enough to show measured outcomes. Wellcome and Nature together is an unusually clean credibility signal for a UK digital therapeutic in mental health, particularly anything with peer-reviewed evidence. The Nature association means judging will lean academic. Pure product entries without a research backbone won't shortlist. Worth a submission if you have a published outcomes paper or active RCT data in anxiety, depression, or psychosis. Skip if pre-evidence.

Clinical evidence
NIHR Health Technology Assessment (HTA) Programme
National Institute for Health and Care Research · Varies, often £500k-£2m+
A commissioned call is open now (closes 2 September) for applications addressing James Lind Alliance priority areas, NICE-flagged research gaps, and themes not well covered in the existing HTA portfolio. The route to fund a definitive clinical trial of your digital health technology. Long timelines (years), academic-led, but produces NICE-grade evidence that unlocks reimbursement. If your near-term need is product evidence, look at i4i Connect or Innovate UK first; HTA is the multi-year play.
