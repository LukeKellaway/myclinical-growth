What goes here

Opportunity tips, general feedback, or bug reports.

Three things land here. Tips are anything we should be tracking but aren’t. Feedback is anything that would make the resource more useful for you. Bug reports are when something on the site or in the brief is wrong or broken. All of it goes to the editor and we read every one.

  • Tip: NHS or wider UK public-sector tenders, frameworks, DPS routes, or grant calls easy to miss
  • Feedback: a feature you want, a category we’re ignoring, a tone that grates, a brief that’s too long
  • Bug: broken links, mis-classified items, stale deadlines, anything the daily brief got wrong
