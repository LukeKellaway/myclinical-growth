Before your first bid

NHS procurement is fragmented across roughly two dozen portals. Each has its own registration, its own qualification questionnaires and its own gotchas. The biggest reason good companies lose bids is missing a hoop they didn't know existed.

I've written a plain-English how-to-apply guide for every major portal. Start with these:

NHS SBS
National framework operator. Runs the £900m Healthcare AI framework. Famous for "stealth releases" where you don't know it dropped.
G-Cloud / Digital Marketplace
The main route for SaaS into the NHS. Application windows open roughly annually. Miss one, wait a year.
Atamis (NHS Health Family)
Closest thing to a single NHS procurement portal. Most trusts post here. Free supplier registration, set your category alerts carefully.
NHS Innovation Service
If you are pre-procurement and want the NHS to take you seriously, this is the front door. Free, structured support, not a sales channel.
