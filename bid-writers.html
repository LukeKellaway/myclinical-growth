Bid & Grant Writing Help

Need help writing your NHS bid or grant submission?

Most NHS procurement or grant submissions are won or lost on the quality of the written response. If you’ve found an opportunity and don’t have the time or the in-house experience to answer it well, get in touch. Tell us about the opportunity and we’ll introduce you to an experienced bid writer.
