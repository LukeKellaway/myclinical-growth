How it works

The data is already free. It just isn't in your inbox!

A £900m framework shouldn't be a stealth release. MyClinical Growth does three jobs so a founder never finds out too late.

01

Aggregated

More procurement portals, framework operators and grant funders than you can shake a stick at. 45 sources and counting, pulled into one place. Find a Tender and Contracts Finder feed in automatically. The rest are tracked for you.

02

Signalled

Signal matters more than the source. Every opportunity gets a plain-English read on what it actually means. Which lots matter, who it's really for, whether the deadline is realistic.

03

Delivered

A daily brief and weekly round up of what's new, meaning you don't miss when something major drops. Browse everything free; subscribe only if you want it to come to you or you can keep coming back to the site as you wish.
